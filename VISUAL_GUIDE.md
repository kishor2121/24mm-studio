  □ Select all images (18+)

## Tips for Best Results

📸 **Organize Uploads**: Do all images of one couple at once
📸 **Consistent Naming**: Use same format (e.g., "FirstName & LastName")
📸 **Feature Best**: Use "Home" section for your best couple images
📸 **Services**: Match service type to event (Wedding, Engagement, etc.)
